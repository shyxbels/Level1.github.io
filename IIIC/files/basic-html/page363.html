                                T3 Teachers’ Capability Enhancement Seminar-Workshop
                                                      July 30, 2019
                                   Venue: International House II, CvSU-Main Campus



                       The T3 Teachers’ Capability Enhancement Seminar-Workshop is an academic
               capability training enhancement usually conducted on a yearly basis in the campus which
               primary goal is to enhance T3 faculty members’ teaching capabilities. This is usually held prior
               to the opening of classes. The highlights of the activity this year were on mental health and
               orientation on academic and administrative policies which was held last July 30, 2019 at the
               Cavite State University-Main
                Campus, International House 1.

                   I.     Morning Session
                               The morning program officially started at 9am with an opening prayer and the
                          singing of the national anthem. Prof. Noel A. Sedigo welcomed all participants
                          and guest lecturers. He also gave brief overview of the topics to be covered for the
                          rest of the day. After the Dean’s talk, the lecture proper commenced. It begun with
                          a one-hour Deep Breathing exercise facilitated by Ms, Amida Chara Rosario N.
                          Wolski, CEO of Radha Kunda Wellness. This particular exercise promotes
                          mental relaxation through context of proper breathing. This was followed by the
                          first lecture on “Understanding Mental Health in the workplace” presented by Ms.
                          Yolanda T. Padilla, Assistant Manager for HR from BBT Online Global, Inc. Then,
                          Ms. Wolski took charged in explaining and demonstrating through another set of
                          exercise on how to be able to develop “Mindfulness for Mental Wellness”. The
                          participants were also subjected to personality check test on mindfulness. The last
                          activity in the morning finished off with a yoga/meditation session for Happiness
                          and Fulfillment in Life led by Mr. Thomas F. Wolski, co-founder and CEO of
                          Nature’s Power Nutraceuticals Corporation.
                              The guest lecturers were then awarded with certificate of recognition and token
                          of  gratitude  then  followed  by  a  photo-opt  with  the  participants.  It  was  a  fruitful
                          morning session indeed having such mind-body relaxing activity. A different and
                          new experience for all faculty members of T3 campuses. The morning program
                          ended exactly 12:00 noon.

                   II.    Lunch Break- 12:01-1:00


                   III.   Afternoon Session
                              After a sumptuous meal during lunch break, the afternoon activity
                          immediately proceeded starting off with the introduction of the key officials of the
                          T3 campuses. Faculty members per department were also introduced by their
                          respective department chairperson.
                              The next part of the program was intended for lectures on the administrative
                          and academic policies. Ms Shaine C. Hayag, HR Coordinator of General Trias
